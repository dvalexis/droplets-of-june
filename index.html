Droplets of June: Excerpts of June Jordan's Poetry

This is an interactive web zine that serves as an archive of poetry from June Jordan. these excerpts are droplets of desire, connection, and unmitigated purpose. may this page be a portal and a mirror into your own wellspring of poetically tender resolve.

This project was built inside Seeda School.

These poems
they are things that I do
in the dark
reaching for you
whoever you are
and
are you ready?
